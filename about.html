ABOUT US

Young Explorers is not just an after-school care provider; it's a haven for young minds to grow, explore, and thrive.
Founded by three passionate mums who recognized the need for a nurturing and engaging after-school environment,
our center is dedicated to providing a safe and enriching space for children during those crucial after-school hours.



MISSION STATEMENT

Our mission at Young Explorers is to create a warm, welcoming, and stimulating environment
where children can unwind, learn, and play. Fueled by the love and dedication of our founders,
we are committed to filling the gap in childcare services, ensuring that working parents have peace of mind,
knowing their children are in capable hands.
